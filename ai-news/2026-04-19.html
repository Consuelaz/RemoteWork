VentureBeat explains the train-to-test scaling paradigm: AI reasoning doesn't necessarily require spending huge amounts on frontier models. Research shows smaller models can achieve stronger performance on complex reasoning tasks by increasing inference-time compute while maintaining manageable per-query costs. This finding is significant for enterprises and developers - by optimizing end-to-end AI compute budgets, optimal balance between performance and cost is achievable without chasing maximum parameters.
