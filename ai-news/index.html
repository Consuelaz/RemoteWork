NVFP4 Post-Training Quantization: Why FP8 Should Become Legacy

NVIDIA Research 发表 NVFP4 后训练量化方案，在仅一次量化感知蒸馏后，与 FP8 精度差距小于 1%，但推理吞吐量提升 2-3 倍。技术规格为 16 元素块大小、FP8 块缩放、二级 FP32 张量缩放，专为 Blackwell 硬件大规模推理设计。这直接挑战了 FP8 作为默认训练和推理格式的地位。论文指出，继续使用 FP8 训练等于双重纳税：既付出了更高的计算成本，又在精度上没有实质优势。FP8 应从默认选项降级为遗留选项，4 位块缩放格式将成为新的行业基准。

NVIDIA Research has published its NVFP4 post-training quantization scheme, achieving less than 1% accuracy gap with FP8 after just one quantization-aware distillation pass, while delivering 2-3x inference throughput improvement. The spec features 16-element block size, FP8 block scaling, and secondary FP32 tensor scaling, purpose-built for Blackwell hardware large-scale inference. This directly challenges FP8's status as the default training and inference format. The paper argues that continuing to use FP8 training is double taxation: paying higher compute costs without material accuracy advantage. FP8 should be downgraded from default to legacy, with 4-bit block-scaled formats becoming the new industry baseline.
