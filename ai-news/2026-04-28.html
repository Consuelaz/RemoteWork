Karpathy Releases 'Eureka' Curriculum Learning Framework: AI Models That Teach Themselves

Andrej Karpathy 开源发布了「Eureka」自课程学习框架，这是一个让 AI 模型自主设计训练任务并持续自我提升的强化学习系统。Eureka 的核心思路是让模型充当「教师」来为自身生成由易到难的训练样例，无需人工设计课程，系统会自动评估当前能力边界并生成针对性的挑战任务。在 Karpathy 的实验中，基于 Eureka 训练的小模型（7B 参数）在数学推理上超越了部分 70B 量级的静态训练模型。他在 GitHub 帖中写道：「这可能是 LLM 从语言模型走向真正学习机器的关键拐点。」项目已在 GitHub 开源，首日获得 15,000 个 Star。

Andrej Karpathy open-sources 'Eureka', a self-curriculum learning framework that enables AI models to autonomously design and generate their own training tasks for continuous self-improvement. Acting as its own teacher, the model evaluates its capability boundaries and generates progressively challenging examples without human curriculum design. Experiments show a Eureka-trained 7B model surpassing some 70B models on math reasoning. Released on GitHub, it garnered 15,000 stars on day one. Karpathy calls it 'the critical pivot from language models to true learning machines.'
