ChatGPT Images 2.0: text rendering breakthrough marks new era for AI image generation

OpenAI 发布 ChatGPT Images 2.0 模型，在图像中生成文字的能力实现了质的飞跃——从两年前 DALL-E 3 连菜单上的 'margaritas' 都拼不对，到现在可以生成完全可商用的真实餐厅菜单。Images 2.0 具备「思考能力」，可搜索网络、从单一提示生成多张图像并自我检查纠错。它支持日语、韩语、印地语等非拉丁文字，可创建营销素材和多格漫画，最高分辨率达 2K。OpenAI 拒绝透露具体模型架构，但从扩散模型转向自回归模型的趋势值得关注。这一突破标志着 AI 生成内容向商业实用化迈出了重要一步，也对 Canva 等设计平台构成了新的竞争压力。

OpenAI released ChatGPT Images 2.0 with a quantum leap in text rendering within images. From DALL-E 3's gibberish like 'margartas' on menus two years ago, Images 2.0 now generates commercially viable, photorealistic menus and documents. The model can search the web, generate multiple images from one prompt, and self-correct. It supports non-Latin scripts (Japanese, Korean, Hindi), creates marketing materials and multi-panel comics, and outputs up to 2K resolution. OpenAI declined to reveal the architecture, but the shift from diffusion to autoregressive models is notable. This breakthrough poses new competitive pressure on Canva and design platforms.

French music streaming platform Deezer revealed shocking data: 44% of new music uploads are AI-generated, with approximately 75,000 AI tracks uploaded daily. Even more striking, 97% of listeners cannot distinguish AI-generated songs from human-created ones. Deezer has developed specialized AI detection technology (false positive rate below 0.01%) and demonetized 85% of AI music streams. CEO Alexis Lanternier says proactive measures have minimized AI fraud. However, tools like Google Lyria 3, Gemini, Suno, and Udio continue to lower the barrier for AI music creation, and watermarks are becoming increasingly easy to remove.
