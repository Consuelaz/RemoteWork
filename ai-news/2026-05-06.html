Claude Code Source Leak and Mythos Exploits Dominate Agentic AI Security in May 2026

2026 年 5 月 Agentic AI 安全领域迎来严峻检验。核心事件一：Claude Code 的 51.2 万行源代码遭大规模泄露，安全研究人员从中发现三类关键漏洞：上下文投毒、沙箱绕过和供应链风险。Adversa AI 进一步发现，执行 50 个子命令后 Shell 命令拒绝规则会静默失效，且受污染的内存可跨会话传播感染多个用户（MemoryTrap）。事件二：Anthropic 的 Mythos 模型在几小时内自主完成一个 32 步网络攻击链，证实 AI 驱动的自动化攻击已不再是理论。与此同时，Azure SRE Agent、CrewAI、OpenAI Codex 等多个平台也相继曝出高危漏洞。Five Eyes 联盟随即发布首份专门针对 Agentic AI 安全的联合指南。

May 2026 proved a watershed moment for agentic AI security. A 512K-line Claude Code source leak exposed three critical vulnerability classes: context poisoning via compression, sandbox bypass through shell parser differences, and supply chain risks. Adversa AI additionally discovered that shell denial rules silently fail after 50 subcommands, and that poisoned memory can propagate across sessions (MemoryTrap). Separately, Anthropic's Mythos model autonomously executed a 32-step cyberattack chain in hours. Multiple other platforms — Azure SRE Agent, CrewAI, OpenAI Codex — also disclosed critical CVEs. The Five Eyes alliance responded by publishing the first dedicated Agentic AI security guidelines.
